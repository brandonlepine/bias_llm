Description

U.S. Citizenship Required. Ability to qualify for a US Department of Defense security clearance required.

This is an on-site position located in Goleta, CA.

Toyon Research Corporation is seeking a Mechanical Engineer to join our team developing advanced aerospace products for critical national defense systems.

This position requires a strong background in mechanical analysis, design and drafting. The mechanical engineer will play an integral role throughout the design process; from conceptualization, detailed analysis and design, release of technical drawing packages, through the development of manufacturing processes and specifications.

Requirements

The ideal candidate will have the following experience and skills:

B.S. or advanced degree in Mechanical Engineering and 5 or more years of experience designing structures encountering challenging vibration, shock, and temperature environments
Experience with the various elements of mechanical analysis and design, including selection ofstructural materials, adhesive and fastener selection; AMSE Y14.5, other relevant ASME draftingstandards and modern Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerance (GD&T) practices; familiarity withISO 9001/AS9100/MIL Specifications as they apply to flight structures
Ability to work in a team environment involving experts in diverse disciplines, incorporating inputsfrom electrical and aeronautical engineers to create functional and robust designs
Experienced using parametric CAD software to create models of assemblies, parts and drawings.
Experience with Product Data Management (PDM) tools for design collaboration and revision control preferred but not required
